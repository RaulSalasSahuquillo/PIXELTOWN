Welcome, Mayor!

Welcome to PIXELTOWN! You are the newly appointed leader. Your job is to build the best city possible, manage resources, construct buildings, and keep your citizens happy.

Beware—if their happiness drops below 10%, a coup d'état might destroy everything you've built!

PIXELTOWN YOUTUBE

Features

  • Build Your Town: Construct simple houses, supermarkets, streetlights, and decorations like the "My Town My Rules" monument.
  • Manage Economy: Earn money from your inhabitants and spend it wisely in the town shop.
  • Keep People Happy: Buy Lov'yc Hand Soap, Gold Face Masks, Shampoo, and Wipes to boost happiness and experience.
  • Interactive UI: Fully built with Python and Pygame, featuring retro pixel art aesthetics.
  • Video Intro: Rendered with MoviePy for an epic cutscene at launch accompanied by an original soundtrack.

How to Play

1. Clone the repository.

git clone https://github.com/rsalas-enetres/PIXELTOWN.git

2. Install the prerequisites:

pip install pygame moviepy

3. Navigate to the code/ folder and run the game:

cd code
python main.py

Follow the on-screen prompts to enter your name and your city's name. Have fun and try not to get overthrown!
